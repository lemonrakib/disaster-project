How often should you send donation letters

Many fundraising professionals would recommend around four direct mail donation letters per year, with one email per month. However, this number will vary depending on both the donor and the nonprofit. A good idea is to send out a survey asking for communication preferences as soon as someone makes a donation or opts into any of your email lists. Then, record that data within your fundraising platform and refer to it when you schedule future correspondences.
